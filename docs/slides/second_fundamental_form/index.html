Second Fundamental Form

Let \(M\) be a regular hyper-surface (\(n\) dimensional submanifold of \(\RR^{n+1}\)) with normal \(N\). The second fundamental form is the field of bilinear forms, \[ A(X, Y) = \ip{D_{\bar{X}} \bar{Y}}{N} \] where \(X, Y\) are tangent vector fields and \(\bar{X}, \bar{Y}\) are any smooth local extensions of \(X, Y\).

Gauss Equation

For convenience, write \(D_X Y\) for \(D_{\bar{X}} \bar{Y}\).

\[ D_X Y = \nabla_X Y + A(X, Y) N \]

Follows from \(T\RR^{n+1} \simeq TM \oplus NM\)

\begin{align*} \nabla_X Y &= \pi_{TM} (D_X Y) \\ \pi_N (D_X Y) &= A(X, Y) N \end{align*}

Weingarten Shape Operator

Weingarten Shape Operator

Weingarten Shape Operator

Weingarten Shape Operator \(W = -DN\)

\[ W : TM \to TM \]

\[ 0 = \partial_X \ip{N}{N} = 2\ip{D_X N}{N} = - 2\ip{-W(X)}{N} \]

Weingarten and Second Fundamental Form

\(A(X, Y) = g(W(X), Y)\)

\begin{equation*} \begin{split} A(X, Y) &= \ip{D_X Y}{N} = \partial_X \ip{Y}{N} - \ip{Y}{D_X N} \\ &= \ip{Y}{W(X)} \end{split} \end{equation*}

Symmetry

\[ A(X, Y) = A(Y, X) \]

\begin{equation*} \begin{split} A(X, Y) &= \ip{N}{D_X Y} \\ &= \ip{N}{D_Y X - [X, Y]} \\ &= \ip{N}{D_Y X} \\ &= A(Y, X) \end{split} \end{equation*}

Computing the Second Fundamental Form

Computing the Second Fundamental Form

Sphere

On the sphere, \(A = g\) since

\[ N(p) = -p \] \[ W = -dN = \Id \] \[ A(X, Y) = g(W(X), Y) = g(\Id(X), Y) = g(X, Y). \]

Local Coordinates

Let \(\varphi : \RR^n \to M\) be a local parametrisation. Then \[ A_{ij} = A(\partial_i, \partial_j) = \ip{\partial_{ij}^2 \varphi}{N} \]

Local Coordinates

Recall \(\partial_i = d\varphi(e_i) = \partial_i \varphi\) \[ D_{\partial_i} \partial_j = \partial_{ij}^2 \varphi \] \[ A_{ij} = \ip{D_{\partial_i} \partial_j}{N} = \ip{\partial_{ij}^2 \varphi}{N} \]

Graphs

For \(M = \graph(f) = \Big\{\big(x^1, \dots, x^n, f(x^1, \dots, x^n)\big)\Big\}\) \[ A_{ij} = \frac{\partial_{ij}^2 f}{\sqrt{1 + \abs{\nabla f}^2}} \]

Graphs

\[ \varphi(x^1, \dots, x^n) = \big(x^1, \dots, x^n, f(x^1, \dots, x^n)\big) \] \[ \partial_i = e_i + \partial_i f e_{n+1} \] \[ N = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 + \abs{\nabla f}^2}}\Big(-\sum_j \partial_j f e_j + e_{n+1}\Big) \]

Graphs

\begin{align*} N &= \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 + \abs{\nabla f}^2}}\Big(-\sum_j \partial_j f e_j + e_{n+1}\Big) \\ \partial_{ij}^2 \varphi &= \partial_i(e_j + \partial_j f e_{n+1}) = \partial_{ij}^2 f e_{n+1} \end{align*} \begin{equation*} \begin{split} A_{ij} &= \ip{\partial_{ij}^2 \varphi}{N} = \ip{\partial_{ij}^2 f e_{n+1}}{N} \\ &= \frac{\partial_{ij}^2 f}{\sqrt{1 + \abs{\nabla f}^2}} \end{split} \end{equation*}

Extrinsic and Intrinsic Curvatures

Let \(M^n \subseteq \RR^{n+1}\) be a hypersurface. Then \[ \Rm(X, Y) Z = A(Y, Z) W(X) - A(X, Z) W(Y) \] \[ \Rm(X, Y, Z, W) = A(Y, Z) A(X, W) - A(X, Z) A(Y, W) \] \[ \nabla_X A (Y, Z) = \nabla_Y A(X, Z) \]

Extrinsic and Intrinsic Curvatures

\begin{equation*} \begin{split} 0 &= \Rm^{\RR^{n+1}} (X, Y) Z \\ &= D_X (D_Y Z) - D_Y (D_X Z) - D_{[X, Y]} Z \end{split} \end{equation*}

Extrinsic and Intrinsic Curvatures

\[ D_Y Z = \nabla_Y Z + A(Y, Z) N \]

\begin{equation*} \begin{split} D_X (D_Y Z) &= D_X \big[\nabla_Y Z + A(Y, Z) N\big] \\ &= D_X (\nabla_Y Z) + D_X[A(Y, Z)] N + A(Y, Z) D_X N \\ &= \nabla_X (\nabla_Y Z) + A(X, \nabla_Y Z) N \\ &\quad + D_X[A(Y, Z)] N - A(Y, Z) W(X) \end{split} \end{equation*}

Extrinsic and Intrinsic Curvatures

\begin{align*} D_X (D_Y Z) &= \big[\nabla_X (\nabla_Y Z) - A(Y, Z) W(X)\big] \\ &\quad + \big[A(X, \nabla_Y Z) + D_X[A(Y, Z)]\big] N \\ -D_Y (D_X Z) &= -\big[\nabla_Y (\nabla_X Z) - A(X, Z) W(Y)\big] \\ &\quad - \big[A(Y, \nabla_X Z) + D_Y[A(X, Z)]\big] N \\ -D_{[X, Y]} Z &= -\nabla_{[X, Y]} Z \\ &\quad - A([X, Y], Z) N \end{align*}

Extrinsic and Intrinsic Curvatures

: Tangential part

\[ 0 = \Rm(X, Y) Z - \big[A(Y, Z) W(X) - A(X, Z) W(Y)\big] \]

Extrinsic and Intrinsic Curvatures

: Normal part
\begin{equation*} \begin{split} 0 &= \Big[A(X, \nabla_Y Z) + D_X[A(Y, Z)]\Big] \\ &\quad - \Big[A(Y, \nabla_X Z) + D_Y[A(X, Z)]\Big] \\ &\quad - A([X, Y], Z) \\ &= D_X[A(Y, Z)] - A(Y, \nabla_X Z) \\ &\quad - \Big[D_Y[A(Y, Z)] - A(Y, \nabla_X Z)\Big] \\ &\quad - A([X, Y], Z) \end{split} \end{equation*}

Extrinsic and Intrinsic Curvatures

\begin{equation*} \begin{split} 0 &= D_X[A(Y, Z)] - A(Y, \nabla_X Z) \\ &\quad - \Big[D_Y[A(Y, Z)] - A(Y, \nabla_X Z)\Big] \\ &\quad - A([X, Y], Z) \\ &= D_X[A(Y, Z)] - A(Y, \nabla_X Z) - A(\nabla_X Y, Z) \\ &\quad - \Big[D_Y[A(Y, Z)] - A(Y, \nabla_X Z) - A(\nabla_Y X, Z) \Big] \\ &\quad + A(\nabla_X Y, Z) - A(\nabla_Y X, Z) - A([X, Y], Z) \\ &= \nabla_X A (Y, Z) - \nabla_Y A(X, Z) \end{split} \end{equation*}
