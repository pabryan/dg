Plane curves

$$
\partial_s \begin{pmatrix} T \\ N \end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix}
0 & \kappa  \\
-\kappa & 0
\end{pmatrix}
\begin{pmatrix} T \\ N \end{pmatrix}
$$

<p class="fragment">
Space curves

$$
\partial_s \begin{pmatrix} T \\ N \\ B\end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix}
0 & \kappa & 0 \\
-\kappa & 0 & \tau \\
0 & -\tau & 0 \\
\end{pmatrix}
\begin{pmatrix} T \\ N \\ B\end{pmatrix}
$$
</p>

<p class="fragment">
