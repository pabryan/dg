$$
\kappa = \lvert \partial_s T\rvert
$$

<p div class="fragment">
Measures the change in angle of the unit tangent - "bending"
</p>

<p div class="fragment">
Straight line: $\gamma(t) = p + t V$, $\kappa \equiv 0$
</p>

<p div class="fragment">
Circle: $\gamma(t) = r(\cos t, \sin t)$, $\kappa \equiv 1$
</p>
