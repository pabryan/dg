---
heading: Regular Curve That Crosses Itself
label: regular_non_simple_curve
---

The parametrisation
$$
\gamma(t) = (t^3 - 4 t, t^2 - 4)
$$
is regular, but it is not one-to-one. That is, it crosses itself.
