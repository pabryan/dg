<p class="fragment fade-in">Velocity Vector: $\gamma'$</p>
<p class="fragment fade-in">Unit tangent: $T = \frac{\gamma'}{\lvert \gamma' \rvert}$</p>
<p class="fragment fade-in">Tangent Line: $t \mapsto  \gamma(t_0) + (t-t_0)\gamma'(t_0)$</p>
