---
heading: Simple Closed Curve
label: curves-simple-closed
---

The parametrisation
$$
\gamma(t) = \left([1 + \cos(5t)/2] \cos(t), [1 + \cos(5t)/2] \sin(t)\right)
$$
is a simple, closed curve.
